Game

Phase 1: Planning
Each player (from left to right) picks three different actions, which will be played in the upcoming round from top to bottom. Once the actions are picked, the “READY” button hides them from other players.
Phase 2: Robbery
Once all the players pick their actions, the “START” button initiates the next round, and performs all the picked actions one by one, displaying them on the screen.
Phase 3: Round End
Once all players performed their actions, the last wagon unhooks from the train, and players standing on it are leaving the game. Closest to the last wagon player gets the $. In case of a draw, player standing on the wagon’s roof gets the $. The first turn is passed to the next player (displayed under the map), and the Phase 1 - Phase 3 repeat.
Game End
Game ends once only one player remains on the train, or all the wagons were unhooked. In that case the player with more $ wins.

Actions

Fire
Shoot the nearest player you are facing. The bullet flies through the wagons till it hits someone. Player can’t shoot someone standing on the roof, while standing inside the wagon and vice-versa. Shot player shifts one wagon in direction of a bullet, and lies down. Lying players can’t be shot, the bullet flies over them. Shot players shifted out of the train leave the game. If the player is shot, instead of performing an action during its turn, the player stands up.
Turn around
Turn your player opposite direction its facing now.
Climb
Move your player to the top or to the bottom of the wagon.
Move
Move your player inside the next wagon, if the player stands inside the wagon, or on top of the next wagon, if the player stands on top of the wagon, in the direction it’s facing. Players moved out of the train leave the game.
